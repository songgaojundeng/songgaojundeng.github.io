Songgaojun (Amy) Deng

I am a Postdoc Researcher in the AIRLab at the University of Amsterdam, work with Prof. dr. Maarten de Rijke and Dr. Sebastian Schelter.

I received my PhD in Computer Science from Stevens Institute of Technology in 2022, supervised by Dr. Yue Ning.

Google Scholar | DBLP | Github | LinkedIn | CV

Research interests

Machine learning and data mining motivated by real-world problems in social, health informatics and e-commerce; domain generalization in time series. My research focuses on enhancing the interpretability and trustworthiness of predictive models.
